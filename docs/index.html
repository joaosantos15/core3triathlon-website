Why & Who

From first laps to finish lines

It started with a few friends, none of us confident in the water, all of us curious about triathlon. With the goal in mind and one friend coaching us, we went from 50m crawling without drowning to completing our first Ironman 70.3 altogether. However the biggest win wasn't crossing the finish line, but the friendships and memories that have been built while training and racing together.

With Core3, we want to offer the same to others: a space to get into triathlon, to train seriously and to have fun.

Train with purpose

  • Events: Technique refinement, pacing strategy, and race simulations.
  • Community: Accountability, encouragement, and fun along the way.
